Know Our Story

We Listened, We Ideated, We Developed, And We Delivered

With decades of experience in the glass industry, we launched Tufftron in 2021. And we had just one goal, to remain as flexible and nimble as possible to satisfy the needs of each of our customers. And we are happy that we could accomplish the goal time and again and still stick to the same goal even after serving thousands of customers.

We came across different people with different mindsets, understanding, and requirements in the last two years. Still, we never considered any of our customers too demanding. We hence left no stone unturned to satisfy their unique needs and choices. Moreover, we never stopped ourselves from making any decision or investment that works in favor of our customers. We have equipped ourselves with state-of-the-art glass cutting and fabrication machinery over time so that no barrier can stop us from providing top-quality glass and glass products to our customers. We also offer glass modification services, including notching, drilling, edging, cutting, and glass tempering.

In the last couple of years, our customers and suppliers domestically and worldwide have recognized us for our quality of glasses and for having a wide variety of thickness and glass types, including toughened glass, heat-strengthened glass, insulated glass, frosted glass, laminated glass and more.

At Tufftron, you are more than just a customer. You are a friend, partner, and member of our family-owned and operated business. Hence, we do whatever it takes to fulfill your requirements and dreams with exemplary services, products, a team of experienced staff, and years of experience. And our turnaround is fast from the quotation to successfully finishing your project.
