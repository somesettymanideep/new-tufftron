Welcome to Tufftron

An aspiration with commitment and hardwork, filled with passion, led to a new beginning into the world of glass processing to enrich its expertise of advanced technologies, collectively called as "TUFFTRON". With an experience of over 4 decades in trading building materials, facilitating state of the art infrastructure spread over 35,000sqft area, the industry is equipped with the newest global technologies, to process a toughened, tempered, treated SAFETY GLASS under the brand name "TUFFTRON", the most durable safety glass. VAJRA SAFETY GLASS is a first of its kind, youth-led, glass processing industry established in the year 2020, located in the core capital 'AMARAVATHI', Andhra Pradesh. It brings TUFFTRON to you to meet the ever escalating demand of architectural & safety glasses in the domestic and international markets. Here at Vajra Safety Glass, we manufacture various kinds of safety glasses like TEMPERED/TOUGHENED,HEAT STRENGTHENED, INSULATED (DGU), LAMINATED, FROSTED/SAND BLASTED glasses. We process "clear/tinted/reflective" and all kinds of LOWe (soft/hard) coated glasses with a huge production capacity i.e, 500sqmtrs per day with thickness from 4mm to 19mm. We look forward to provide our best services to achieve 100% customer satisfaction with our highly qualified, trained and experienced technicians indulged in delivering the finest quality to experience the excellence.
