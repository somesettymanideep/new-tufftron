LAMINATED GLASS

Established in 2020, Tufftron is a one-stop destination for individuals, companies, and architects for their laminated glass requirements. With the belief that customer satisfaction is the only factor for a company’s growth, we have garnered a vast customer base that continues to grow day by day. We employ individuals who are dedicated to their respective rules and put that much effort into achieving the shared vision of our company and each unique goal of our customers.

Laminated glass has a safety inner layer or film namely, polyvinylbutyral resign glue between 2 or more Tempered/ toughened glass sheets , which holds the glass in place in the event of breaking .The glass fragments keep binding on the thin film,only creating a spider web crack pattern,ensuring safety .we use several layers around the film such as float glass , colored glass , tempered glass, heat-reflecting glass, heat – absorbing glass, and more in making a laminated glass. And the layer quantity we offer is up to 9,to match the selectivity of client’s requirement.

  • Canopies
