EXTENDING THE BOUNDARIES OF GLASSMAKING WITH OLEO GLASS MANUFACTURING

Tufftron has been an Indian-owned and operated glass manufacturing company since 2020, catering to architects, contractors, engineers, and automobile industries both locally and regionally. With over two years of experience and a team of highly skilled engineers and designers at all levels, we are continuously striving to be the best oleo glass manufacturer and supplier in India. Our objective is to supply our oleo glass and other value-added products at the shortest lead time and competitive pricing while maintaining the best quality standards. With the capability of small to large production lines running simultaneously, we have become glass solution specialists for engineers and architects who require glass products in some set specifications. We are comprised of state-of-the-art tools, machinery, and technology to produce premium and personalized oleo glass and glass products that suit business and residential needs.

Our oleo glass is a blind integrated system inside an insulated glass unit that remote controls and a magnet can operate. The integrated blinds are available in Venetian and pleated variety and come in a wide range of colors. Now you can eliminate the need to clean windows and the blind systems as our oleo glass is completely protected from dust.

Use Of Tufftron Oleo Glass:

  • Facades: Tufftron oleo glass in the exterior facades provide more light and an excellent ambiance to your buildings. Our oleo glass facades offer numerous advantages such as ease of maintenance and cleaning, transparent views, and more to make your buildings look stunning and modern.
  • Partitions:Tufftron oleo glass partitions give you a sense of definition and differentiation without affecting the openness of your office—separate different areas in your office entirely without restricting light and lines of sight.
  • French Windows: We manufacture large oleo glass panes for french windows to allow much more light. We take so much effort to maintain the neat look of our oleo glass so that you can achieve an excellent environment for your home’s interior and exterior without having the hassle of regular cleaning.
  • Doors: Tufftron manufactures different oleo glass doors to bring safety and beauty to any architectural unit. Our oleo glass is a perfect choice for regions that experience thermal stress and heavy wind loads.

Advantages of Tufftron Oleo Glass:

  • It is highly durable and resilient to impact and, therefore, less likely to shatter upon breakage.
  • Tufftron oleo glass offers five times better aesthetic appeal to your buildings and reduced maintenance needs compared to ordinary glass.
  • Windows and doors manufactured with oleo glass can protect you against harmful UV rays since they can absorb up to 65% of the UV rays which fall on their surface.
  • There are various colors you can choose to get better solar control inside your home.
