INSULATED GLASS(DGU)

Insulated Glass, often called double glazing, is a combination of two or more panes of glass paced apart with a spacer bar and hermetically sealed with a primary and secondary sealant to form a single unit with one or more air spaces in between. IG units improve the thermal performance, thus significantly reducing heating and air-conditioning costs. IG units also reduce interior condensation in cold climates, and increase comfort near windows, thus maximizing the usable interior space

At Tufftron, we rely on automated solutions and the best engineers to reduce costly mistakes and improve the overall efficiency of our insulated glass-making process.
