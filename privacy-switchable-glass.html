MAKING PRIVACY ATTAINABLE EVERYWHERE WITH PRIVACY SWITCHABLE GLASS

Whether you have a meeting in the boardroom, want to conduct a personal interview, or need some time in solitude, Tufftron privacy switchable glass puts all such desires in control, with the privacy switchable glass. Our privacy switchable glass or switchable smart glass is made with polymer dispersed liquid crystal technology (PDLC), which is commonly used for providing privacy on demand. We use a film of liquid crystals glued between 2 panes of glass. When even a tiny amount of electricity passes through the film, those liquid crystal molecules align to allow the incident light to pass through it, and the glass appears transparent. The same privacy switchable glass turns opaque when the electrical supply is removed, and the liquid crystal molecules relax.

Our privacy switchable glass is manufactured in-house using the latest machinery and furnaces with a team of highly efficient engineers and designers. It is assembled in a climate-controlled and clean environment. To ensure consistency and reliability, every unit of privacy switchable glass we produce undergoes rigorous quality checks, including electrical tests, automation, and more. We offer a wide range of thicknesses, colors, and sizes, and all our privacy switchable glasses are bulletproof and have high-temperature resistance.
