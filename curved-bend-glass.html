CURVED/BEND GLASS

Curved glass is has been reheated and shaped, usually over a metal mould, to a desiredbend or curvature, and left to cool in that position. Curved glass can be foundinarchitectural designs, from glass domes and hand rails to revolving doors and displaycases. This can be used as “curved tempered” curved insulated and curved laminated for moreimproved safety.

Special And Unique Applications Of Tufftron Curved Glass:

Over the last few years, we have pioneered many innovative architectures with our curved glass across India. For the environmentally friendly properties of our curved glass and the ability to retain high light transmission, it is an ideal choice for architects for:

  • Glass Domes: Tufftron curved glass is prominently known for its ability to bring uniqueness to any architectural design. We have supplied our glass to many clients to use it glass externally for glass domes and other architectural applications.
  • Spiral Staircase:Utilizing Tufftron curved glass in interiors is an excellent non-conventional alternative, and it has high load resistance. Our curved glass has also been used internally for spiral staircases in many residential and commercial spaces.
  • Glass Capsule Elevator: Utilizing glass in the architecture of capsule elevators is a contemporary trend. The transparency of our curved glass brings a sense of space and allows natural light to pass through. For being affiliated with a minimalist look, our curved glass has also been used for capsule elevators in residential and commercial buildings.
  • Vehicle Windshield: Being made with 100% shatter and splinter-proof technology, Tufftron curved glass has been used to make vehicle windshields.
  • Glass Roof: Since it allows many shapes and avoids optical distortions, Tufftron curved glass has also been used for glass roofs, and it is capable of creating aesthetically pleasing roofs with soft curves.

Advantages Of Tufftron Curved Glass:

  • Tufftron curved glass is an aesthetic winner with the flexibility and flow of unprecedented creativity in the design of a structure.
  • The curvature of the glass panel makes it highly load resistant, and the arched structure minimizes deflection.
  • Due to its inherent transparency, Tufftron curved glass enriches a building with natural light allowing light to enter and travel through a building or structure.
